The Midfielder Academy

The Midfielder Academy is first private football academy, providing football courses alongside academic studies for aspiring footballers who are aged 15 or older. The courses we offer range from one week to three years and combine specialist football training with first-class education. The quality of the academy has seen former students succeding at professional football clubs within the UK, Europe, USA or at a significantly higher level in their home country, with many going on to represent their national teams..l
